Account Setup

Step 1 – Create Account

Register or log in to your Easy Mobile Pay account.
Make sure your details are correct.

Step 2 – Start Mining ₦170,000

Tap the Start Mining button to begin.
Each click increases your balance by ₦34,000.
After five clicks, your total balance becomes ₦170,000.

Step 3 – Purchase Activation Code

Pay ₦6,500 inside the app to purchase your activation code.
After payment, you’ll receive a message containing your EMP Code.

Step 4 – Input EMP Code

Enter your EMP code in the field provided.
Once verified, your withdrawal button will be activated.

Step 5 – Withdraw Earnings

Click Withdraw Now to send ₦170,000 to your linked wallet or bank.

Make sure you input your correct EMP code before withdrawal.
